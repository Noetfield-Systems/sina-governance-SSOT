## Completeness critic — what the catalog missed

**Strongest pick (critic):** DLM → P0-PGR packet bridge: pipe DLM's MACHINE_VALIDATABLE / ADVISOR_REVIEW cluster output straight into p0pgr_campaign_planner_v1.py's compile_packet() as DRAFT, lint-only packets. It is the single biggest surface the whole catalog missed — DLM's classified, plain-English, de-duplicated decision items ARE the "problem statement" the P0-PGR packet compiler already consumes, so a thin adapter fuses the system's two flagship brains into one messy-backlog→dispatchable-work pipeline. Fully phase-safe (DRAFT status, linter-only, dispatch_now stays false), grounded in two live scripts, and turns two isolated demos into an actual assembly line.

**Verdict:** The catalog is strong on per-subsystem enforcement/harness gaps but systematically under-weights cross-subsystem wiring (it treats all 10 subsystems as silos), governance-fence hardening inside DLM, protection of live commercial surfaces, and the deterministic differential tests that would prove its own offline reimplementations faithful — the highest-leverage plays sit precisely in those seams.
