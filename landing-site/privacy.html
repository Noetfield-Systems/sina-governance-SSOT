Privacy Notes

This standalone UI does not persist raw operational payloads by default. Receipts and lead forms are retained only for manual follow-up when you submit contact details.

Any live API integrations can be configured with environment controls for retention, export, and deletion windows.

  • Contact form fields are stored with timestamp and source only for manual sales qualification.
  • No hidden tracking beyond local intent telemetry for UX optimization.
  • Receipt artifacts are generated by the runtime lane under separate governance controls.
