Proof and control

Commercial proof you can verify

PASS/FAIL comparison

Each run ends with machine-readable outcomes. Teams can compare successful and recovered steps before accepting the final output.

  • PASS: contract-compliant and replayable
  • PASS_WITH_WARN: accepted with controlled fallback
  • FAIL: needs escalation and re-run policy

Failure & redo path

Recovery is explicit, not hidden: a failed partition is isolated, retried with corrected policy, then replay-verified before receipt emit.

  • Timestamped checkpoints
  • Retry event and failover trace
  • Evidence hash for audit export
