# Commercial Domain Strategy (v1)

**Status:** `LOCKED` (part of `PRODUCT_CATEGORY_MAP_LOCKED`) · **Locked:** 2026-07-09T07:06:11Z · **Authority:** SG-owned
**Source of truth:** [`PRODUCT_CATEGORY_REGISTRY_v1.json`](PRODUCT_CATEGORY_REGISTRY_v1.json) → `categories[].domain_strategy`

> This document records **intended** domain posture per category. It authorizes **no domain purchase and no public claim.** Per the founder lock rules, **every** category's posture *right now* is **umbrella-internal**: no standalone domain, no public SKU, no marketing surface until its activation receipt exists.

## Principle

- **Now (all categories):** `umbrella-internal`. The category lives inside the platform, unbranded publicly, governed by canon. No domain is bought, no page is published.
- **Target (per category):** the eventual commercial home, unlocked only by the category's activation receipt:
  - **umbrella** — lives under `noetfield.com` / `sourcea.app` as a capability, never its own domain.
  - **subdomain** — earns a subdomain (e.g. `studio.sourcea.app`) once proven.
  - **standalone-later** — earns its own commercial domain / SKU only **after** its activation receipt.

## Canon guardrails (override this document on conflict)

- Noetfield's **public** face is 3 locked SKUs (Trust Brief, Copilot Governance Pack, Bank Pilot). None of these 10 categories may be published as a 4th public Noetfield SKU without founder ratification.
- **"Noetfield never touches value."** No domain/page for any category may imply custody, settlement, payment routing, or money movement — including TrustField.
- Ventures that already own domains (TrustField `trustfield.ca`, SourceA `sourcea.app`, WitnessBC `witnessbc.com` cutover pending) keep them as **vertical proof lanes**, not as claims that a platform category is commercially activated.

## Activation sequence (intended, not scheduled)

1. **RepoGraph L0 (CAT-02)** is closest to a defensible standalone (already live-running) — gate: measured token-tax + second-repo receipts.
2. **Studio IDE (CAT-08)** earns a subdomain once its loop is green.
3. **P0-PGR (CAT-03)** and **Receipt Layer (CAT-09)** remain umbrella spines (sell the platform, not the part).
4. **Factory Lines / Sandboxes / Workflow / Memory** stay umbrella-internal until their runtime receipts exist.
5. **App Builder** requires a scope decision (distinct product vs Studio mode) before any domain thinking.
6. **Vertical lanes** already carry their own domains as ventures — but a live domain is **not** a claim of commercial activation; each still needs a revenue receipt.

- ❌ Publishing any category as a public product/page/SKU.
- ❌ Any claim that a category is "available", "launched", or "for sale".
- ❌ Any implication that Noetfield or TrustField touches, holds, or moves value.
