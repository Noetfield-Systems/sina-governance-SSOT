# SKILL_06_COMMERCIAL_CONSEQUENCE_SIMULATOR

## Purpose
Make agents reason commercially, not just technically.

## Questions
1. Could this reduce trust?
2. Could this confuse buyer vs partner audience?
3. Could this remove a conversion path?
4. Could this reduce founder credibility or control leverage?
5. Could this expose private materials?
6. Could this create more manual work?
7. Could this reduce candidate quality?
8. Could this hurt LinkedIn/outreach conversion?

## Output
- commercial risk
- patch to reduce risk
- verification signal
