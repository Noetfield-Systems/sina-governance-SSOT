# PATTERN — ANTI-THEATER

**Status:** Pattern + factory integrity law.
**First written:** 2026-07-03 12:34 PDT

## Definition
The system cannot fake success — flaws are blind-detected and heals are independently verified; no self-certification.

## The law (the factory's own integrity)
Flaw-projection must be **blind to the healer**; the heal must be **independently verified**; both leave **receipts**. If the healer knows where the flaw is, the heal proves nothing (grade-its-own-homework).

## Projected, not seeded
Flaws are PROJECTED (existing rot surfaced — stale rules, dead pipelines, claims no longer true), including **rule-as-poison** (a stale rule that has itself become the disease). Not fabricated theater.

## Proving ground / receipts
The verify→gate loop; the demonstrated malformed-bundle → FAIL → gate-refused → live-unchanged. Blind-detected flaw → independent FAIL/PASS → live-unchanged-on-FAIL, logged.

## Why it's the product
You cannot sell "anti-drift/anti-theater" on a promise — you sell it on signed receipts showing a blind-detected issue was independently caught. **The provability is the product.** The substrate IS the pattern-proving engine.

## Relation
= negative-proof + mechanical-not-prose + author≠subject, packaged as a sellable pattern.
