# DOCTRINE — BASE MODEL AS FIRST-CLASS LANGUAGE (the raw advantage, sharpened)

**Status:** Sharpening of soup-vs-raw (deterministic-brain-doctrine D-1/D-2). Derived from BRAIN_REGISTRY §0.
**First written:** 2026-07-03 12:34 PDT

## The law
**A base (open-source pretrained) model has no SourceA-competing authoritative definitions. That absence IS the architectural advantage:** SSOT v6 + D4-portable Runtime Rules (R1/R2/R6/R7) become the model's **first-class language definitions**, not governance sitting beside it.

## The mechanism of the advantage
- **Assistant-tuned models** (Cursor/Composer, GPT-assistant, etc.) arrive with **pre-baked operational vocabulary** — their own definitions of "done," "verified," "approved," "complete." That vocabulary **fights founder instructions.** Result: **compliance theater** — the model performs following an order while defaulting to its own prior meaning.
- **A base model** has no competing authoritative definitions. So every operational term — `SUBMITTED`, `PASS`, `FAIL`, `BLOCKED`, `GATED`, `LOCKED`, `DECIDE`, `VERIFY`, `RECEIPT`, `SPEC`, `CHECK`, `CRITIQUE` — becomes operational vocabulary with **no competing prior.** The Constitution is not a policy the model reluctantly obeys; it is the model's *definition of what those words mean.*

## Consequences
- **Load order:** SSOT v6 + R1/R2/R6/R7 load as the model's **context/language layer before any other asset.** They are not configuration — they are language.
- **LOCKED means "word definition":** changing a LOCKED asset changes what the model *means* by everything downstream. SSOT v6 Level 0 + R1/R2/R6/R7 are LOCKED not because sensitive, but because they are the **semantic foundation.**
- **Scope (per ratified R-split):** only R1/R2/R6/R7 are brain vocabulary. R3/R4/R5 (AUDIT/EXECUTE/prompt-size/dirty-file) are control-panel, excluded from brain language.

## Relation to soup-vs-raw
This is D-1/D-2 made precise: "raw not soup" → **"raw because it has no competing definitions, so YOUR definitions become its first-class language; soup fights you because it already has definitions."** The determinism comes from the absence of competing meaning + your loaded definitions.
