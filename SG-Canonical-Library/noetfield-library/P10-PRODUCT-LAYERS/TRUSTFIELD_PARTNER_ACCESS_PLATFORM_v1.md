## What it is

TrustField offers **two partner surfaces** on one domain:

1. **Commercial partner programs** — MSBs, MSPs, and consultancies run branded intake, TF program IDs, checklist workflow, and API exports with licensed partners as settlement party of record.

2. **Partner Access OS** — gated acquisition funnel for strategic contributors, operating leads, and director-track candidates (apply → email verify → NDA → briefing room).
