# THE GAP DOCTRINE — Strategy SSOT v1
*Canonical decision frame. Reference it; do not re-derive it.*

---

## 0. Why this exists
To terminate the repeating young-lawyer / old-lawyer debate. Once a move is priced
by this doctrine, the framing is **BANKED**. It reopens only on a *new fact* — a rule
wakes up, an enforcement route opens (e.g. the June 2025 private-action change in
Canadian competition law) — **never on re-argument.** Re-litigating a settled frame
is the oscillation bug. Banned.

---

## 1. The terrain — THREE zones, not two
- **WHITE** — rule clear, you comply. Commodity. Everyone can stand here → **zero edge.**
- **GRAY** — no rule, ambiguous rule, or asleep rule. Asymmetry **without** tail.
  **The only zone that pays.**
- **BLACK** — rule clear, awake, you'd violate it. Asymmetry **with** an unpriced tail.
  - *Dormant black* (rarely enforced) vs *live black*.
  - Inside dormant: **cheap-trigger** (an adversary fires it in one click — a verifiable
    false fact) vs **expensive-trigger** (costly/unlikely to fire — the speeding trade).

> Failure mode to kill: collapsing **gray** and **dormant black** into one word.
> Gray makes you rich. Dormant black buries people who mistook a sleeping rule for a
> dead one. Sleeping rules wake up.

---

## 2. The thesis — proven, not asserted
**Edge lives in the gap because it is the only place with asymmetry and no tail.**

Your own moves are all gap-plays:
- speeding → **enforcement gap**
- copying winners → **IP gap** (model/funnel/pricing carry no rule; patents do)
- networking / connection → **relationship gap** (unlegislated)
- anchoring / leverage → **cognition gap** (outside every rulebook)

Scaled, named proof: tax **avoidance** vs evasion; the **Eurodollar market**;
**Uber/Airbnb** category-ambiguity; **card counting**. The largest wealth engines
are gray — not white, not black.

Mechanism: White = no asymmetry (commodity). Black = asymmetry + tail (blows up).
Gray = asymmetry − tail. **Alpha = asymmetry − tail. Only gray holds both halves.**

---

## 3. The roles — a loop that must CONVERGE, not oscillate
- **YOUNG LAWYER / Rule-mapper** → what the rules say. Output: *the map.*
- **OLD LAWYER / Gap-mapper** → where rules are absent / ambiguous / asleep, and where
  they are awake + verifiable. Output: *the terrain.*
- **TRADER / Pricer** → prices the move and caps the floor. Output: *the decision.*

The loop **terminates at the trade.** Returning to the rulebook after the trade is
priced is the bug. Convergence is the deliverable.

---

## 4. THE TRADE TEST — apply in 30 seconds
1. **Is there a rule at all?** No → white/gray, free, take it.
2. **Is the rule clear?** Ambiguous / unclassified → **GRAY** — your edge. Move fast,
   before it classifies you.
3. **Clear rule you'd violate → is the trigger cheap & verifiable for an adversary?**
   - **Cheap / one-click checkable → −EV, refuse.** (Verifiable false facts live here.)
   - **Expensive / unlikely + floor capped → conscious priced trade** (the speeding
     trade). Take it eyes-open.
4. **Always, before the trade:** who is the *real* adversary, and have I denied him a
   clean artifact? Cap the floor structurally — numbered co, insurance, asset wall —
   **before** the event, not after.

---

## 5. Standing tactics — the anti-post-mortem kit
- **Launder fact into opinion.** Falsifiable → unfalsifiable. ("5.0 from 47 reviews"
  is a checkable number; "trusted by West Side homeowners" is legally air.)
- **Spend specificity only where bulletproof.** Vague everywhere else. A precise lie
  is a timestamped confession.
- **Disclaim the category, not the content.** "Sample / illustrative" changes the
  legal species without killing the persuasion.
- **Sequence, don't choose.** Claim-light page now → harvest real proof in weeks →
  claim-heavy version once it's true. Market now *and* proof later, no tail between.
- **Build the floor before the event.** Write the ceiling of the post-mortem in advance.
- **Harden against the real adversary only.** The competitor with a screenshot — not
  the phantom regulator.

---

## 6. The one standing refusal — by arithmetic, not morality
A **verifiable false statement of fact with a cheap trigger** is black with a one-click
detonator: tiny upside, real tail, adversary cost ≈ 0 — and the compliant version is
usually available for almost nothing. **−EV every time.**

Canonical instance: the fake CPO# when a real licensed operator's cert sits right there
for free. The doctrine refuses it on the math. No morality required.
