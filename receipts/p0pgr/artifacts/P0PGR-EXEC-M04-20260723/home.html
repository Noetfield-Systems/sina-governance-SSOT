The operational problem

Compliance work is spread across spreadsheets, shared drives, email, and calendars

FINTRAC creates daily recurring work: program governance, KYC/KYB evidence, case preparation, and report packages. Dual-obligated teams then face RPAA operational risk, safeguarding, and annual-report evidence in parallel — usually in different tools.

That fragmentation makes basic questions hard:

  • –Where is the compliance program record and latest policy evidence?
  • –Where is this case in the workflow?
  • –Who owns the next action?
  • –Who approved FILE vs NO-FILE?
  • –Are STR / LCTR / LVCTR / EFT packages review-ready?
  • –Where is the FINTRAC confirmation?
  • –Can we export the evidence trail for examination or RPAA annual prep?
  • –What can we safely automate next?

TrustField organizes payments compliance operations in one auditable workspace. Your organization remains the Reporting Entity; custody and settlement stay with your existing providers.

FINTRAC program foundation

What a compliance examination would ask to see — built as recurring workflow

The first product wedge is FINTRAC. These four modules form the program foundation before RPAA expansion.

  • Compliance program governance

    Policies, officer records, program health, tasks, and review schedules.

  • KYC / KYB evidence workflows

    Beneficial ownership and customer evidence organized for review — not a proprietary identity product.

  • Report preparation with audit trail

    STR, LCTR, LVCTR, and EFT preparation with structured fields, narrative drafts, and human gates.

  • Risk assessment

    Inherent risk → controls → residual risk, with evidence and review history.

Natural expansion: RPAA operational risk, safeguarding, incident management, controls, evidence, and annual-report preparation — for dual-obligated companies already operating FINTRAC workflows here.

The FINTRAC case workflow

Alert → case → review → human approval → submission tracking → evidence closure

  1. 1

    Alert or report candidate

    Bring a monitoring hit or report candidate into a durable case with an owner and SLA clock.

  2. 2

    Data collection

    Collect missing or conflicting data against a clear checklist before drafting.

  3. 3

    Analyst review

    Prepare the narrative and package under dual-control gates — without unsupervised filing.

  4. 4

    Human approval

    Authorized officers record FILE / NO-FILE / ESCALATE. TrustField does not auto-file.

  5. 5

    Submission tracking + evidence

    Track confirmation and close with an exportable evidence pack for supervisory review preparation.
